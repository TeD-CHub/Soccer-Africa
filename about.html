About Us & Schools

Who We Are

At Soccer Africa, we believe in the endless potential of every child. Founded by legendary professionals, our academy focuses on early development, bridging raw talent with world-class technical coaching, tactical awareness, and sports science.

We are more than just a football club. We are a family dedicated to shaping disciplined, educated, and elite young athletes ready for the global stage.

  • UEFA & CAF Licensed Coaches
  • State-of-the-Art Facilities
  • Elite Player Pathways
  • Academic Support & Mentoring

School Partnerships

We believe education and sports go hand-in-hand. Soccer Africa proudly partners with local primary and secondary schools across the region to bring high-quality soccer development directly into the educational curriculum.

By working with schools, we identify young talents early and provide them with the infrastructure to thrive both academically and athletically without compromise.

  • Integrated after-school programs
  • Equipment and coaching provided
  • Academic milestone requirements for players
  • Inter-school league organization
