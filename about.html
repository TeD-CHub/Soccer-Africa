About Us & Schools

Academy Overview

Soccer Africa Football Academy is a youth football development institution committed to nurturing young football talent, promoting discipline, improving physical fitness, and creating opportunities for young players to grow both on and off the pitch. The academy focuses on holistic player development through professional coaching, mentorship, teamwork, and competitive football exposure.

The academy aims to become one of the leading grassroots football development centers in Kenya and Africa by identifying and developing talent at an early age.

Mission & Vision

Mission

To develop talented young footballers through professional training, discipline, education, and mentorship while promoting health, teamwork, and positive character development.

Vision

To become a leading football academy in Africa that develops future professional players, responsible citizens, and leaders through sports.

Our Objectives

  • To identify and nurture football talent among young players.
  • To provide professional football training and development programs.
  • To promote discipline, teamwork, leadership, and sportsmanship.
  • To create opportunities for players to participate in local and international competitions.
  • To support the physical, mental, and social wellbeing of young athletes.
  • To build pathways for players into professional football, schools, colleges, and national teams.
  • To promote community engagement and youth empowerment through sports.

Core Values

  • Discipline
  • Respect
  • Teamwork
  • Integrity
  • Excellence
  • Commitment

Academy Activities

  • Fitness and conditioning programs
  • Community sports outreach
