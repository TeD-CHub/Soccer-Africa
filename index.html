Official FKF Affiliation & Girls Unit Launch

Bernard Omondi, FKF Chairman Nairobi West, graced our fun day. Officially affiliated the academy with the Federation.

In addition, he provided the academy with five balls, received by the academy CEO, Coach Arnold Omondi dedicated to starting a girls unit in the academy. Registration on-going.
